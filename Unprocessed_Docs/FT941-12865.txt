
Japan's unemployment rate rose to 2.9 per cent in December from 2.8 per cent
the previous month, the highest level in more than six years, the
government's Management and Co-ordination Agency said early today, Reuter
reports from Tokyo. At the same time, the jobless rate for 1993 as a whole
reached its highest level in five years, averaging 2.5 per cent, up 0.3
points from 1992.
