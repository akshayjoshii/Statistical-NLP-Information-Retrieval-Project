
In the Brea-Olinda Relays: 
Woodbridge, which won five relays, took the girls' championship with 83 points, ahead of Thousand Oaks (40) and Canyon (26). Woodbridge's Kaci Keffer won the 400 (57.44) and Brea-Olinda's Nancy Chabot won the 100 hurdles (15.65). La Habra's Tim Mahon won the boys' 1,600 (4:23.99).
