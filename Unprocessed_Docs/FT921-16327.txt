
House prices fell by an average 1.2 per cent in December, according to the
Nationwide monthly house price index. The fall compares with a 1 per cent
rise in the November index. Over the past year, house prices have fallen by
2.2 per cent. The average house sold in December cost Pounds 56,626 compared
with Pounds 57,479 a year earlier and Pounds 59,272 in June 1991.
