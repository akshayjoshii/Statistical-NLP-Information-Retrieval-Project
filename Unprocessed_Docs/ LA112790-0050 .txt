
Gribin Von Dyl Realtors and National Escrow Corp., based in Sherman Oaks, have been purchased by First National Realty Associates of Norwalk, Conn. 
Terms of the deal, completed Nov. 19, were not disclosed. 
First National Realty will operate eight branch offices in Southern California with more than 300 employees, Gribin Von Dyl President Harvey Dlugatch said. First National Realty, which is a real estate brokerage firm, is also the parent of Ramsey-Shilling Inc. and Dippell Realty, both based in Los Angeles.
