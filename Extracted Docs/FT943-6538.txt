Pickpockets have become such a menace to air travellers that Amsterdam's Schiphol airport is hosting a Pan-European conference on how to foil pickpocket gangs . Airport authorities from Frankfurt , Brussels , and London are expected to attend . Jan Pijnenburg , head of Schiphol airport 's crime prevention department , said it is unclear how many gangs are operating at European airports but added that they cause significant damage . Most gangs comprise four or five members and typically make off with between Dollars 5,780 and Dollars 11,560 ) a day .
