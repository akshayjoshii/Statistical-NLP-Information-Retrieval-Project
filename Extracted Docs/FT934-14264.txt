By now most of you will surely have discovered the compulsive appeal of Cracker ( ITV , 9.00 ) . This series about Fitz , the burly criminal psychologist ( Robbie Coltrane ) , has it all - a flawless script , superb acting , and fine camera-work . There is a touch too much gore for queasy stomachs but these days that appears to be unavoidable . Addressing the theme of evil , Panorama ; The Sinful Messiah ( BBC 1 , 9.30 ) investigates religious guru David Koresh and his cult of the Branch Davidians in Waco , Texas , and the siege and subsequent tragic confrontation . Tales from Borges ( BBC2 , 12.00 ) is a season of films based on short stories by the great Argentinian writer , Jorge Luis Borges . Will To Win : Out Of Africa ( BBC2 , 7.40 ) looks at the sporting achievement of countries like Kenya and Uganda . Sporting victories give a positive image to African peoples but successful athletes often find they have to emigrate because of government corruption and interference in sport .
