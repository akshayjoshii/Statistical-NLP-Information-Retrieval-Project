While Manchester United seek stock-market flotation , Sheffield Wednesday , who beat them in Sunday 's Rumbelows League Cup final , might turn to the FT Ordinary index to appreciate just how long it had been since they last won a Wembley final . It was 1935 , the year the Ordinary was launched . On Sunday , the index ( base 100 ) stood at 1980.1 . We are told that the 1935 Wembley programme cost one old penny . Sunday 's cost Pounds 3 - a 720-fold increase .
