Itamar Franco , thrown unwillingly into the role of Brazil 's president following the resignation two years ago of the disgraced Fernando Collor , has the same personal greyness more often associated with John Major . But he could be forgiven for being irritated with the Uruguayan newspaper El Pais . When he visited Montevideo last weekend , El Pais referred to him as Jose Sarney - who left office in 1989 .
