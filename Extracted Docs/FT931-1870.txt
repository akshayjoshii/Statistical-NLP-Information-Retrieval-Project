Russian and US nuclear submarines collided in Arctic waters this weekend but the crash , 170 km off Russia 's northern coast , caused little damage to either craft , officials said yesterday , Reuter reports from Moscow . Russian naval commanders accused the rival boat of carrying out dangerous manoeuvres . Defence Department officials in Washington said the US regretted the incident . 'The high command of the Russian military fleet expresses its extreme concern over the latest incident of dangerous manoeuvring by foreign submarines in military training zones , ' a Russian navy statement said . A spokesman said the Russian craft suffered minimal damage and there was no radiation leak .
