My Sister Pam : Pam Dawber , who made her television series debut with Robin Williams in the old `` Mork & Mindy '' sitcom , will star in a new half-hour series for CBS-TV . Dawber signed a development deal with the network to star in the as yet untitled series to be produced by Pony Productions in association with Grant-Tribune Productions .
