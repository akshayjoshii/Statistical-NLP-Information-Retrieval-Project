Right-hander Rafael Valdez pitched a perfect game Thursday night , the third in California League history , as the Riverside Red Wave beat the Reno Silver Sox 2-0 in a class-A game at Riverside . Valdez ( 10-5 ) struck out eight . The 21-year-old from the Dominican Republic threw three balls to four batters , including a 3-0 count to one . He struck out three of those batters and got the other to pop out . Right fielder Tim McWilliams ended the game with a diving catch of Brian Bosco 's looping fly ball . McWilliams went 4 for 4 . The Red Waves scored their runs in the sixth inning on consecutive one-out singles by McWilliams , Guillermo Velasquez and Mike Humphreys , and a sacrifice fly by Isiah Clark .
