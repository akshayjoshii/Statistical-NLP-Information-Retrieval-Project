Changes at US Sprint Parent : United Telecom said it will exercise its option to buy GTE 's remaining 19.9 % stake in what originally was a 50-50 joint venture that created US Sprint in July , 1986 . The firm now owns 80.1 % of US Sprint , which has become the nation 's third-largest long-distance carrier . Shareholders at United Telecom 's annual meeting in Kansas City , Mo. , also overwhelmingly approved changing the parent firm 's name to Sprint Corp. to capitalize on the subsidiary 's more widely recognized name .
