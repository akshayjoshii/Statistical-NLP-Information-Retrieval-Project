A North Hollywood woman was seriously injured Wednesday when she lit a large firecracker she recovered from a trash bin , believing it was a candle , fire department officials said . Estella Rica , 54 , suffered burns to her right hand and left foot , Los Angeles City Fire Department officials said . Rica told paramedics she had recovered the two-inch firecracker from a plastic bag in a dumpster behind her Victory Boulevard apartment . Rica told paramedics that she was in the kitchen of her apartment when she lit the firecracker . As the fuse began to hiss , she dropped the firecracker to the floor and it exploded . Rica was taken to St. Joseph 's Medical Center in Burbank , where spokeswoman Alexis Parkes said a plastic surgeon was attempting to reconstruct Rica 's badly damaged right hand . The blast did not damage Rica 's apartment , a Fire Department spokesman said .
