The Orange County Hazardous Materials Team , administered by the county Health Care Agency 's division of environmental health , responds to calls on chemical spills and other incidents involving hazardous materials . Sometimes the team goes to the site and other times it advises citizens or local fire departments over the phone . There were 29 spills recorded in HAZMAT 's response logs in September . The following details the seven spills that occurred in the Costa Mesa/Newport Beach/Irvine area of the county .
