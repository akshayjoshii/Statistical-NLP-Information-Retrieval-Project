BOC , the industrial gases group , has moved quickly to plug the gap left by Phil Aiken 's surprise move to be chief executive of BTR Nylex in Australia . Barry Beecroft , 48 , who has been with BOC for 26 years , has been appointed managing director of the group 's European gas business , which has annual sales of over Pounds 500m . Beecroft , a chemical engineer , is currently regional director of BOC 's gas business in the South Pacific and managing director of its Australian gas operation . He was formerly operations director of BOC 's UK gases business .
