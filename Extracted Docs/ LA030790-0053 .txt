Eduard A. Shevardnadze will visit Africa this month in the first trip by a Soviet foreign minister to the continent , the government announced in Moscow . Shevardnadze will visit Zambia , Zimbabwe , Mozambique , Angola and Namibia , the Foreign Ministry said . He may meet Secretary of State James A. Baker III during the March 21 visit to Namibia , formerly South-West Africa , a former German colony ruled by South Africa since World War I , the announcement said .
