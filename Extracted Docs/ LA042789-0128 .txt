Mitek Systems , a manufacturer of laser printers and facsimile machines built to TEMPEST security standards , reported second-quarter net income of $ 265,000 on sales of $ 5.4 million , up from a net loss of $ 508,000 on sales of $ 2 million in the like quarter one year ago . Net income for the six-month period ended March 31 was $ 22,000 , contrasted with a net loss of $ 1.1 million for the like period the previous year .
