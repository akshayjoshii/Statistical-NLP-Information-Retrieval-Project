Probe , winner of the first heat , and Park Avenue Joe , winner of the second , raced to the first dead heat in a Hambletonian race-off Saturday at the Meadowlands . Park Avenue Joe , however , was declared the winner because he finished second in the first heat . Probe broke stride and finished ninth in the second heat . Park Avenue Joe is trained by Chuck Sylvester , who also won the Hambletonian in 1987 with Mack Lobell . Park Avenue Joe won $ 565,500 from a purse of $ 1,131,000 . Probe won $ 282,750 . Park Avenue Joe led throughout the race-off but , nearing the wire , Probe appeared to have a slight lead . Stewards called for two prints of the photo finish and deliberated for several minutes before declaring the race for 3-year-old trotters a dead heat . The time for the one-mile race-off was 2:00 2/5 . The last quarter was trotted in a fast :26 3/5 .
