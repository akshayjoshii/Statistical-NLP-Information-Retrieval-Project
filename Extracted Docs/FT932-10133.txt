ERICSSON , the Swedish telecommunications group , said yesterday it had won a DM750m ( Pounds 303m ) order from Mannesmann Mobilfunk to supply equipment to the company 's digital mobile telephone network in Germany , writes Hugh Carnegy in Stockholm . The order is the biggest Ericsson , the world 's leading supplier of digital mobile telephone equipment , has received for mobile systems . Mannesmann 's D2 network is one of two German digital systems linked to the evolving pan-European mobile telephone network known as GSM .
