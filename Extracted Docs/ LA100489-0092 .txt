Mayor Donna L. Chessen has urged city residents to help the people of hurricane-ravaged Charleston , S.C. , with donations of money for food , clothing and other items . During Monday 's City Council meeting , Chessen made a public plea for donations to help thousands of Charleston residents who were left homeless and hungry in the wake of hurricane Hugo . Hugo has been called the costliest storm ever to hit the United States . Chessen said donations can be sent to the Charleston Disaster Relief Fund , City Hall , 800 Broad St. , Charleston , S.C. 29401 .
