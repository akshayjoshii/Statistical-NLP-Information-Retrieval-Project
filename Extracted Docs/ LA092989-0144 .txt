State Sen. Art Torres ( D-Los Angeles ) will lose his driver 's license Oct. 10 because he refused to be tested for alcohol when Sacramento police officers stopped his car earlier this month , the Department of Motor Vehicles reported Thursday . The Sept. 6 incident marked Torres ' second drunk driving arrest in Sacramento County . He is on probation for the first incident in 1987 . In the most recent case , a Sacramento police officer said Torres , 42 , was driving without lights and was arrested for drunk driving . According to the officer , Torres refused to submit to chemical tests to determine his blood alcohol level .
