David Duke , the former Ku Klux Klan leader elected last month to the Louisiana House of Representatives , called the Rev . Jesse Jackson a `` racist '' and said affirmative action and minority set-aside programs should be abolished . `` I think the racists in this city are people like Jesse Jackson , '' Duke said at a Chicago news conference after addressing the national convention of the right-wing Populist Party of America . Jackson declined comment on Duke 's statements , except to say : `` I do n't think it 's an American point of view . ''
