AMERICAN Express Receivables Financing has filed with the US Securities and Exchange Commission to securitise certain of its credit card receivables , Reuters reports from New York . The deal will be the first by the company , part of the American Express group . American Express said its Lehman Bros brokerage unit would serve as underwriter for any securitisation transaction . Revolving credit receivables excluded from the proposed deal are American Express ' Optima credit card , an American Express spokeswoman said .
