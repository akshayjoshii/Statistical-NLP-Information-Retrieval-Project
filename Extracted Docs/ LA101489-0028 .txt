El Camino Real 14 , Chatsworth 0 -- Jamal Anderson scored twice and rushed for a game-high 96 yards in 15 carries to lead El Camino Real ( 2-2 , 1-1 ) at Chatsworth . The senior fullback scored on runs of 10 and one yard . In the second quarter , El Camino Real drove 90 yards for its first touchdown . Anderson accounted for 51 yards in seven carries and scored on a 10-yard run . A stingy El Camino Real defense held Chatsworth to just 11 yards passing and 109 yards in offense . Chatsworth ( 1-2-1 , 0-2 ) was assessed 85 yards in penalties . HANDEL WHITMORE
