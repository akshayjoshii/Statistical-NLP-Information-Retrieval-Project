The world 's highest stock exchange in Lhasa , capital of China 's Tibet region , has seen its highest daily turnover of Yn5m ( Dollars 588,000 ) since it opened in January . The stock exchange has more than 2,000 members in a city of just 180,000 . The exchange is a member of China 's two stock exchanges in Shanghai and Shenzhen and trading is linked .
