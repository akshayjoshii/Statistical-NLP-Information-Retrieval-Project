In a dramatic shift , the Port Hueneme City Council moved veteran Councilman Orvene Carpenter into the mayor 's job Wednesday night , ousting Dorill B. Wright , mayor for nearly 17 years . Although the council removed Wright from the board 's top job , members voted 4 to 0 to name him `` mayor emeritus . '' The honorary title will take effect when Wright leaves the council . Ken Hess was elected mayor pro tem . DONNA DOWLING
