ABC broadened its relationship with its highest-rated star Thursday , signing Roseanne Barr and her husband , Tom Arnold , to produce a TV movie , a special and a series for the network . She will star in the movie and special , but the series is not intended to feature her , the network said . All three will be produced by the couple 's Barnold Productions . `` Her success has contributed greatly to our success and we 're delighted to extend that partnership to new creative areas in the future , '' ABC Entertainment President Robert Iger said .
