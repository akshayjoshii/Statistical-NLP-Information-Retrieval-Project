A medical examiner testified today that William Reedy 's blood-alcohol level was well above the legal limit when the pickup truck he is accused of driving crashed and former major-league manager Billy Martin was killed . Onondaga County Medical Examiner Erik Mitchell told a Town of Fenton Court jury that Reedy 's blood-alcohol level as measured at a hospital immediately after the crash was 0.16 % . The legal limit in New York for driving while intoxicated is 0.10. %
