El Salvador 's leftist guerrillas have admitted for the first time that hundreds of their number have been killed in their five-week-old offensive . Radio Venceremos , the guerrillas ' mouthpiece , said 401 fighters of the Farabundo Marti National Liberation Front have been killed , among them a high-ranking officer named Dimas Rodriguez . The government estimates that up to half of the 6,000 to 10,000 rebels have been killed or wounded .
